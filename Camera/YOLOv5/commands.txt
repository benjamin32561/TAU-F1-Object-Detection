without changing model architecture:
python train.py --img 640 --batch 16 --epochs 1 --weights route_to_weights_file.pt --data route_to_dataset_file.yaml --hyp route_to_hyp_file.yaml

with changing model architecture:
python train.py --img 640 --batch 16 --epochs 1 --weights route_to_weights_file.pt --data route_to_dataset_file.yaml --hyp route_to_hyp_file.yaml --cfg models/model.yaml
